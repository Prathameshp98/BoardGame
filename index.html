    Gameplay

  • The game requires a minimum of two to four players, and with the commencement of the game each player will be given Rs 1,000/-
  • Players will take turns to roll the dice and whoever gets 6 on the dice first can start moving ahead. Other players will have to wait in the starting point till they also get 6 on the dice they roll.
  • The players play around the board to make as much money as possible.
  • Players who are at loss will be a loser and the player who has all the money will be declared the winner.

    • Property

  • In the game, each city is a property. If a player reaches a city either can buy it or leave
  • When a player lands on the property owned or purchased by another player, the player will have to pay rent to the player who owns the property. The player who owns the property can also build a house and charge more rent for properties which have similar colour.

    • Lottery

  • If Player lands on this place, some amount of money will be credit to the balance.

    • Jail

  • If Player lands on this place, a bail amount of 150/- will be charged.
